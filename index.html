MY BIOGRAPHY

My name is Anisiobi Kosisochukwu Cynthia. I graduated from Nnamdi Azikiwe University (Unizik), Awka, Nigeria, with a second class upper division honors in Computer Science in 2023. During my studies, I served as the Executive President of the Nigeria Association of Computing Students (NACOS), Unizik chapter, where I developed strong leadership and organizational skills. My professional experience includes several key roles. I worked as a Project Manager at PeachBlossoms Ltd., where I coordinated teams of designers, developers, and stakeholders. I managed risk assessments and used tools like Trello to keep projects on track and within budget.


I also served as the Deputy Managing Director at Sambros Enterprise from March 2022 to December 2022. In this role, I assisted with strategic decision-making and oversaw daily operations to ensure efficiency. Building and maintaining strong client and partner relationships was a crucial part of my job. In the summer of 2022, I was a Virtual Assistant Team Lead for Tech4dev’s Women in Tech program. I managed daily operations, assigned tasks, and set priorities, which helped me enhance my organizational and leadership skills. From April 2021 to November 2021, I worked as a Social Media Manager at Genesys Tech Hub. My responsibilities included developing and executing social media strategies, managing the company’s presence on platforms like Instagram and Twitter, and engaging with our online community.


Currently, I am involved in the HNG internship as a Product Manager for Team Bingo and also serve as the Assistant Sub-Team Lead of the Documentation and Research team. I’ve been responsible for researching and creating detailed tickets outlining tasks and features needed for our project. I also wrote a Product Requirements Document (PRD) for Remote Bingo, detailing user stories, feature descriptions, and technical specifications. This document played a key role in guiding our project and was submitted as part of our project documentation. My diverse experience has equipped me with skills in digital marketing, social media management, project management, and product management, which have been valuable in my contributions to the HNG internship and my professional growth.
